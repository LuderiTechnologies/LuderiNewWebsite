We're Luderi. This Is Our Story.

Our Mission

We build intelligent tools for prosthetics clinics, so clinicians can spend less time on documentation and admin work to create more time helping people regain movement and quality of life.

Our Philosophy

We believe prosthetic care should be personalized, efficient, and accessible. Built by researchers and engineers from Stanford, KTH, ETH and SSE. Luderi brings AI to the frontlines of care. Eliminating guesswork, lowering costs, and raising the standard for patients everywhere.

Our Solution

We're building the next generation of prosthetic software by combining cutting-edge AI with deep clinical expertise. Grounded in research and driven by ambition, Luderi is designed around the real needs of prosthetic clinicians. Our intuitive platform supports them at every step of the process. Reducing admin, minimizing errors and denials, and becoming a trusted companion in their daily workflow.

Built with clinicians, for clinicians.

The Hidden Cost of Inefficient Tools

Prosthetic clinicians play a vital role in helping people regain movement, dignity, and independence. Their time and expertise are incredibly valuable, yet much of it is lost to outdated software and administrative overload.

Instead of focusing on patient care, they're stuck navigating disconnected systems, filling out paperwork, and rewriting documentation for insurance. It's not just frustrating, it's a waste of talent.

At Luderi, we believe prosthetic clinicians deserve better. Our tools are built to reduce admin, streamline fitting and documentation, and give clinicians their time back so they can focus on what matters most: delivering life-changing care.

Because when clinicians are supported, patients thrive.

"Once I understood the complexities of the administrative and insurance workflow, I saw a clear opportunity. We could apply AI to that entire chain, enabling much higher first-time acceptance rates and cutting down the frustrating delays for patients."

- Viktor Lado Naess, Founding Engineer & Head of AI
