Product Overview

Transform Your P&O Clinic

Luderi is a comprehensive SaaS platform designed specifically for P&O clinics. Our AI-powered solution automates insurance documentation, streamlines component selection, and simplifies procurement processes, allowing CPOs to focus on what they do best - patient care.

  • AI-generated clinical notes
  • Integrated patient & clinic management
  • Smart component selection & ordering
  • Automated insurance processing
  • Real-time analytics & reporting

Technical Specifications

Platform Requirements

  • Browser: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+
  • Internet: Minimum 3 Mbps connection
  • Devices: Desktop, tablet, and mobile compatible

Security & Compliance

  • Encryption: HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit and at rest via Supabase
  • Compliance: HIPAA, GDPR, SOC 2 Type II
  • Authentication: Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
  • Backup: Automated daily backups through Supabase PostgreSQL database

AI & Machine Learning

  • Model: Custom-trained AI assistant for P&O clinical documentation and insurance
  • Accuracy: 95%+ accuracy in clinical note generation
  • Training: Continuously updated with new data
  • Languages: English (additional languages coming soon)

Integration Capabilities

  • APIs: RESTful APIs for custom integrations and third-party systems
  • EHR Systems: Compatible with major electronic health record platforms
  • Insurance: Direct integration with major providers
