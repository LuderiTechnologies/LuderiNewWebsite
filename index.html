What is Luderi?

Luderi is a SaaS platform that beleives in the power of AI to transform the way clinics operate. We belive CPOs spend too much time on burdons the're not able to focus on what they do best. Helping clinicians connecting patient evaluations to the correct components and automating the insurance path in the same process is the key to freeing up time for patient care.

Challenges Clinics Face

Manual documentation overload

Clinicians spend hours writing notes and forms after each visit.

Fragmented processes

Patient records, insurance, and ordering all happen in different systems.

Trial-and-error fitting

Finding the right components requires guesswork and back-and-forth.

What Luderi Offers

AI-generated clinical notes

We automate documentation based on patient input and clinician workflow.

Integrated patient & clinic management

Handle patient data, insurance, and billing in one place.

Smart component selection & ordering

Recommend and order components directly through the platform.

What Led
Us
Here

01 Research roots +

Luderi began as a research project at Stanford, exploring how data and materials science could improve prosthetic outcomes. But when we started speaking with clinicians, it became clear: the real problem wasn't just technical, it was systemic. Legacy tools, admin overload, and fragmented workflows were slowing everything down. We shifted our focus from optimizing parts to empowering the people who use them.

02 Clinician-driven +

We work side by side with prosthetists to understand their daily challenges. From fitting and documentation to ordering and approvals. Their input shapes everything we build. By designing with their workflow in mind, we reduce friction, increase precision, and help clinicians spend more time on what truly matters: delivering lifechanging care.

03 Built for real-world use +

We're not just building features, we're building tools that hold up in fast-paced clinical environments. Every product decision is tested against real workflows, stress-tested in clinics, and refined with clinician feedback. The result: a platform that's not only smart, but simple, stable, and ready for daily use.
